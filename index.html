Notice Anything Interesting?
Rates of international travel from U.S. gateway airports have steadily increased over the last 25 years. Despite a significant decrease in travel in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic, scheduled international services for 2024 show a complete rebound in international travel, and then some.
